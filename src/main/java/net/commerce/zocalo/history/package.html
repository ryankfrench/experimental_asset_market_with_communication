Display trading history to the user. {@link net.commerce.zocalo.JspSupport.TradeHistory} currently displays all transactions, and will eventually provide sorting and filtering choices. {@link net.commerce.zocalo.history.CostAccounter} calculates the costs of bought and sold shares, and gains or losses. The other classes here are a start on a new framework for recording trade history.
