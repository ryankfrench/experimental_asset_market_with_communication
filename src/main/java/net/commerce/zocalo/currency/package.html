These classes represent conserved currencies that form the basis for exchange. {@link net.commerce.zocalo.currency.Currency} is the base class for money (represented by {@link net.commerce.zocalo.currency.Funds}) and tradable {@link net.commerce.zocalo.currency.Coupons}, which represent the assets in a prediction market.

{@link net.commerce.zocalo.currency.CashBank} and {@link net.commerce.zocalo.currency.CouponBank} are the issuers of Funds and Coupons respectively. They provide the ability to control the issuance of these currencies. In the capability sense, they should be "closely held", to ensure that the authority to issue new valuable currencies isn't misused.

{@link net.commerce.zocalo.currency.Accounts} manage cash and coupons for users.
