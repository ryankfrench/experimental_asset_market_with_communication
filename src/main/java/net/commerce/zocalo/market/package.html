These classes implement the market and process orders. {@link net.commerce.zocalo.market.Market} accepts orders entered by a {@link net.commerce.zocalo.user.User} and processes them, either against other orders stored in a {@link net.commerce.zocalo.market.Book}, or against the {@link net.commerce.zocalo.market.MarketMaker}. Separate subclasses represent {@link net.commerce.zocalo.market.BinaryMarket BinaryMarkets} and {@link net.commerce.zocalo.market.MultiMarket MultiMarkets}. In parallel, the MarketMakers are subdivided into {@link net.commerce.zocalo.market.BinaryMarketMaker BinaryMarketMaker} and {@link net.commerce.zocalo.market.MultiMarketMaker}.
